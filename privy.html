Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CrazyReelow.com (the "Website"), operated by CrazyReelow (the "Artist"), collects, uses, and protects your personal information in compliance with:

  • South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)
  • Global standards for data transparency (e.g., GDPR).

By using this Website, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

a. Automatically Collected Data

The Website uses Google Analytics 4 (free tier) to gather anonymized insights about visitor behavior, including:

  • IP addresses (automatically anonymized by GA4)
  • Device/browser details (e.g., screen resolution, OS).
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored.

b. User-Provided Data

  • Contact inquiries via social media links (e.g., name, email).
  • Commission requests (processed externally; no data stored on the Website).

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Google Analytics

To analyze traffic and improve user experience. Data is aggregated and anonymized

2.2 Contact Data

Solely to respond to inquiries about custom artwork commissions.


To the Artist’s knowledge, NO data is sold, shared, or monetized with third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

GA4 Cookies

First-party cookies (e.g., _ga, _gid) track user sessions anonymously. These expire after 2–24 months

No non-essential cookies are used.

4. Data Protection Under POPIA

We adhere to POPIA’s 8 conditions for lawful processing:

  • Accountability: The Artist ensures compliance.
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes.
  • Data Minimization: Only necessary data is collected.
  • Retention: GA4 data is retained for 14 months (default)
  • Security: Technical measures (e.g., encryption) protect data.
  • Transparency: This policy discloses all practices.
  • User Rights: You may request data access/deletion (see Section 5).
  • Cross-Border Transfers: GA4 data may be processed globally but is anonymized 7.

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

You may:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Correct inaccuracies.
  • Demand deletion of your data (contact via social media links).

Note: GA4 anonymizes data; thus, individual requests may not apply to aggregated analytics.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4: Processes data under Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Social Media Platforms: External links (e.g., Instagram) follow their own policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.
